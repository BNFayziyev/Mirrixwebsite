Privacy Policy

How we collect your personal information

We collect personal information through multiple channels, including: website forms (such as quote or contact requests), cookies and similar tracking technologies, email correspondence, and offline interactions (for example, phone calls or in-person discussions).

What personal information we collect

Depending on your interaction with Mirrix Corp, we may collect: name, company name, email address, phone number, message content submitted through our forms, IP address, device/browser data, and basic website usage analytics.

How we use your personal information

We use your information to respond to requests, provide and improve our services, communicate with you about shipments or support, maintain security and prevent fraud, and (where permitted) send marketing communications.

How, why, and what personal information do we share with third parties

We may share limited personal information with trusted third parties that help us operate our business (for example: website hosting, analytics providers, communications tools, and operational service providers). We share only what is necessary to provide services, improve performance, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal information.
